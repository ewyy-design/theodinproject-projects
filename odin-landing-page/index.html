Welcome to Singapore!

Singapore is a sunny, tropical island in Southeast Asia, off the southern tip of the Malay Peninsula. With four major communities in Singapore, we offer different perspective of life in terms of culture, religion, food, language and history.

Interesting facts about Singapore.

It's a city of (man-made) waterfalls.
Singapore pioneered the first F1 night race.
It's one of the world's greenest cities
The Lion City may actually have been inspired by a tiger.
